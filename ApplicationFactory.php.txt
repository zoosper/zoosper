     1	# Phase 0.19 ApplicationFactory wiring notes
     2	
     3	Phase 0.19 does not require new core controller mappings. Keep controller providers in modules.
     4	
     5	Make sure these services are already registered in `ApplicationFactory` from previous phases:
     6	
     7	```php
     8	$services->set(AdminViewRenderer::class, new AdminViewRenderer($adminTemplateRenderer, $adminLayout));
     9	$services->set(AdminComponentRenderer::class, new AdminComponentRenderer($adminTemplateRenderer));
    10	```
    11	
    12	When refactoring the heavy controllers, inject `AdminViewRenderer` from module-owned `config/controllers.php`, not from `ApplicationFactory` direct controller mapping.
    13	
    14	Example:
    15	
    16	```php
    17	PageAdminController::class => static fn (ServiceContainer $services): PageAdminController => new PageAdminController(
    18	    $services->get(SessionGuard::class),
    19	    $services->get(CsrfTokenManager::class),
    20	    $services->get(PageRepository::class),
    21	    $services->get(SiteRepository::class),
    22	    $services->get(PageRenderer::class),
    23	    $services->get(AdminLayout::class),
    24	    $services->has(AdminViewRenderer::class) ? $services->get(AdminViewRenderer::class) : null,
    25	),
    26	```
    27	
    28	The exact constructor change should be applied inside the relevant module controller only.
